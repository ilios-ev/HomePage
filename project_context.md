## 5. Technical Specifications: The 7-Axis System
The "ilios Standard" is built on a 7-point inspection protocol that ensures total vehicle reliability:
1.  **Battery & Charging Axis:** Voltage drops, SOC%, terminal integrity, and charger safety.
2.  **Motor & Controller Axis:** Wiring stability, solenoid health, and error code clearance.
3.  **Steering & Suspension Axis:** Alignment (Toe/Camber), lubrication, and shock absorber testing.
4.  **Braking Axis:** Response time, fluid levels, and parking brake calibration.
5.  **Wheels & Tires Axis:** Pressure, tread depth (min 1.5mm), and rim integrity.
6.  **Chassis & Body Axis:** Structural tightening, roof/column stability, and exterior condition.
7.  **Accessories & Safety Axis:** Lights, reverse alarms, dashboard functionality, and USB ports.

## 6. Smart Ecosystem Tools (Logic Definitions)
The website will feature interactive tools to demonstrate technical authority and provide value to users.

### A. Battery State of Health (SOH) Calculator
*   **Goal:** Estimate battery life remaining based on field performance.
*   **Logic:** 
    *   `SOH = [(Actual_Range_Covered / Rated_Range_New) * 0.7] + [(1 - (Voltage_Drop_Under_Load / Max_Allowable_Drop)) * 0.3]`
    *   *Simplified Version:* `SOH (%) = (Current_Discharge_Time / Nominal_Discharge_Time) * 100`.
*   **Inputs:** Battery Type (Lead Acid/Lithium), Nominal Voltage, Voltage after 1km run, Total Distance on full charge.

### B. Maintenance ROI Calculator
*   **Goal:** Show B2B clients the financial benefit of proactive maintenance vs. reactive repairs.
*   **Logic:** 
    *   `Annual_Savings = (Avg_Breakdown_Repair_Cost * Fleet_Size) - (ilios_PM_Subscription_Cost) + (Downtime_Opportunity_Cost)`.
    *   `ROI (%) = (Annual_Savings / ilios_PM_Subscription_Cost) * 100`.
*   **Variables:** 
    *   Reactive repair costs typically include 30% higher parts cost and 50% more downtime.
    *   ilios PM extends battery life by approx. 25-30%.
